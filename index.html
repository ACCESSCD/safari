Currency: Tanzanian Shilling (TZS). USD is widely accepted, but bills must be crisp and dated 2013 or newer. Tipping guides/staff is customary.

🦟 Malaria Reminder

Antimalarial medication is recommended for the whole trip regardless of itinerary. Tanzania's malaria is chloroquine-resistant. Confirm the right antimalarial with your doctor before departure.

Tips: Use DEET repellent, wear long sleeves at dusk/dawn, sleep under provided nets.
